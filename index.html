Frank Austin Nothaft

Frank Austin Nothaft is a MS/PhD student in Computer Science at UC Berkeley. His research uses large scale commodity computing systems to process scientific data. Frank works with Professor David Patterson in the AMPLab and the ASPIRE lab, and is supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Frank has been an IC Design engineer at Broadcom Corporation since 2011. Frank's work at Broadcom focuses on mixed-signal design automation. Before coming to Broadcom, Frank completed his Bachelors of Science with Honors in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University, and was advised by Professor William J. Dally.

In his spare time, Frank engages in the hammer throw, photography, hiking, and biking.
